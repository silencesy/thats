Passionate about media? Come work at a growing company that offers opportunities to advance and work alongside an accomplished, dynamic team!

Urbanatomy Media is a multi-media company based in top-tier cities across China, with a comprehensive portfolio of products from print magazines, city and national websites, mobile and tablet apps, strong social media presence, as well as regular supplements.

Our publications, That's Magazines and Urban Family, cover a wide variety of topics in Shanghai, Beijing, Guangzhou, Shenzhen and China, including: News, Restaurants & Dining, Bars & Nightlife, Arts & Entertainment, Events, Lifestyle, Families & Kids, Fashion, Culture, Sports, Music, Business, Environment, Technology and much more.

Deeply connected with the local market and culture, we have been in business in China for nearly 20 years. With a vibrant mix of local and international staff, we are focused on delivering qualit y English-language editorial content across a variety of different platforms.

We’re looking for enthusiastic candidates to fill the following positions...

Marketing Executive

The Marketing Executive will report directly to the Head of Communications.

Requirements:
• Bachelor’s degree in advertising, marketing or related program preferred
• Proficiency in English (speaking, reading and writing)
• Basic office computer skills
• Excellent presentation and communication skills
• Ambitious for the media industry and energetic
• Previous work experience in media or digital marketing a plus

Responsibilities:
• Develop and maintain partnerships and sponsorship opportunities
• Manage social media campaigns across various platforms
• Oversee directory of listings
• Perform copywriting and translation work in both English and Chinese
• Assist Events Team to design, plan and implement PR/promotional activities
Conduct marketing research, build and maintain the market intelligence (MI) and regularly provide market analysis reports
• Coordinate closely with the internal sections, especially the sales and editorial sections for maximum synergy

Interested in applying?
To apply, send an email with your CV and cover letter to hr@urbanatomy.com with the subject 'Marketing Executive.'

Business Development (BD) Manager

The BD Manager will be responsible expanding Urbanatomy Media's business field in a large region or in multiple product lines (eg. new digital products).

Requirements:
• Bachelor's degree or above, with a major in advertising or marketing preferred
• Proficiency in English (speaking, reading and writing)
• Basic office computer skills
• Excellent presentation and communication skills
• Ambitious for the media industry and energetic
• Previous working experience in the media industry is a plus

Responsibilities:
• Liaising with relevant internal departments and creating national sales team for a specific territory
• Conducting market research and assessing investment opportunity of nationwide commercial projects, leading company through due diligence exercise, market and financial analysis
• Taking initiative to grow sales revenue by multi-city deals, meeting and exceeding individual/team sales performance and development goals
• Managing prospects and sales forecasts in a specific territory nationwide

Interested in applying?
To apply, send an email with your CV and cover letter to hr@urbanatomy.com with the subject 'BD Manager.'

Editorial Interns

Calling all aspiring editors, writers, journalists and bloggers. Fancy getting hands-on editorial and writing experience with a glamorous English-language media company internship? That’s and Urban Family magazines are looking for interns with an interest in journalism and new media to work alongside our dedicated and dynamic team.

We're looking for enthusiastic candidates with excellent writing and reporting skills and an eye for details.

Requirements
• Basic writing and reporting experience
• Excellent English-writing skills, with a strong grasp of spelling, grammar, punctuation and style
• Experience in maintaining and creating content for a blog or website
• Experience with or interest in learning how to work with common Content Management Systems (i.e. Wordpress, Tumblr, Drupal, etc)
• Experience with or interest in learning HTML basics
• Experience with or interest in learning how to work with common social media platforms (i.e Facebook, Twitter, WeChat, Instagram, Weibo, etc)
• Experience with or interest in learning how to work with Adobe Photoshop or other image editing software (i.e. Lightroom, GIMP)
• Organized, detail-oriented and able to meet deadlines
• Proficiency in Mandarin writing, reading and speaking not required, but would be a plus

Responsibilities
• Writing, copyediting and fact-checking of content for our websites and print magazines
• Researching and pitching new story ideas
• Conducting interviews and getting in touch with appropriate PR, event and media contacts
• Representing That’s and Urban Family at events

Here are some of the benefits you’ll get from interning with us...
• Gain experience in online, mobile app and print magazine publishing
• Learn more about editorial production, digital content management, social media, photography, graphics and more
• Become more familiar with the news publishing cycle and gain a better understanding of proper editorial judgment for both web and print
• Attend networking, media and PR events
• Build up a portfolio of writing clips under your own byline

Interested in applying?
To apply, send an email with your resume, cover letter and three writing samples to Bridget (bridgetodonnell@urbanatomy.com) with the subject 'Editorial Internship.'

Interns should be available to work at minimum 2 days per week.
